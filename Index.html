// 001 — The Concept

Music lives
in the cloud.
Not your pocket.

Zero Space is a personal cloud audio platform that stores your music — every rare track, DJ mix, and local recording that no streaming service carries — and delivers it straight to your ears through a lightweight wearable gateway. Your phone stays home.


The platform handles everything: upload, transcode, store, stream. You just press play on your wrist.

// 003 — Features

Built different.
By design.

Multi-Device
💻
One Account. Every Device.
Your entire library syncs in real time across phone, tablet, PC, and smartwatch. Log in anywhere. Pick up exactly where you left off — always.
  • Simultaneous multi-device login
  • Real-time library sync
  • Continuous playback hand-off
  • Unified playlist and history
Multi-Bluetooth
🎧
Multiple Bluetooth. One Tap Switch.
Connect several Bluetooth devices simultaneously. Zero Space routes audio to the right one based on your active mood profile — automatically.
  • Multiple BT devices connected at once
  • Instant audio routing between devices
  • Per-device volume memory
  • Auto-switch on mood change
Smart Cache
⚡
No Signal? Still Playing.
Smart Cache pre-loads your favourite playlists to the wearable over WiFi at home. Signal drops mid-run? Seamless switch to local cache. You'll never notice.
  • Auto pre-load on home WiFi
  • Invisible stream-to-cache fallback
  • Adaptive bitrate for weak signals
  • Learns from your listening habits
Personal Library
🗂️
Your Music. Not Theirs.
Upload anything. Rare tracks, DJ mixes, voice memos, original recordings. If it's audio, it belongs in Zero Space. No catalogue gatekeeping. Ever.
  • MP3, WAV, AAC, FLAC support
  • Auto metadata extraction
  • Playlist creation and organiser
  • Full-text search across library
Security
🔒
Encrypted. Isolated. Yours.
Every file is end-to-end encrypted and stored in your personal isolated storage space. No other user can ever access your library. No data is sold. No exceptions.
  • End-to-end encryption per file
  • Per-user isolated storage
  • Authenticated streaming only
  • Zero data sold to anyone
Wearable
⌚
Your Wrist Is the Remote.
Full playback control from your smartwatch. Play, pause, skip, volume — all without touching a phone. The wearable is the experience, not a companion app.
  • WearOS native app (Phase 1)
  • watchOS coming Phase 2
  • LTE + WiFi connectivity
  • Full playback controls on wrist

Schedule moods by time of day. Zero Space adapts to your life — not the other way around.
